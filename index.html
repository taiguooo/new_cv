About Me

My name is Tai Guo, born and raised in China. I'm currently a Master's student at Stanford University, studying materials science but having a strong passion about software development.

Before Stanford, I studied nanosciences for my undergrad in both China and Canada. More specifically, I worked on nanoelectronics. My undergrad thesis is titled Fabrication of Nanoscale Interdigitated Array Electrodes for Electrical Measurements on Nanomaterials.
My program was a collaborative program between Beijing Jiaotong University in China and University of Waterloo in Canada, so I spent my first two years in Beijing while taking UWaterloo courses (when UWaterloo profs came to China), and then moved to Canada for the last two years and finally earned two degrees from both universities.
